About Vyasa Puja

Vyasa Puja is the most auspicious occasion to honor and glorify the spiritual master, who represents the disciplic succession dating back to Lord Krishna Himself. On this sacred day, disciples from all corners of the world offer their heartfelt prayers, gratitude, and devotion at the lotus feet of His Holiness Radhanath Swami Maharaj.

This year marks the 75th appearance day of Maharaj, a milestone celebration of his divine mission to spread the teachings of Srila Prabhupada and inspire countless souls on the path of bhakti-yoga.
